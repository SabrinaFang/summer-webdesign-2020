Arizmendi Bakery

Arizmendi Bakery is a worker-owned cooperative specializing in
morning pastries, artisan breads and gourmet pizza.
It's located in the Inner Sunset, just two blocks from Golden Gate Park.
You can also find us in Oakland, Emeryville, San Rafael, and Valencia.

TOGO/Window Service Only: Order in our right window and pick it up from our "doorway counter". Customers will be expected to wear masks and observe social distancing at all times.
